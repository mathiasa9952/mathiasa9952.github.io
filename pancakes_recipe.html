Good Old-Fashioned Pancakes

Servings: 4 | Prep Time: 5 min | Cook Time: 10 min

Breakfast American Quick & Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups all-purpose flour
  • 3 1/2 tsp baking powder
  • 1 tsp salt
  • 1 tbsp white sugar
  • 1 1/4 cups milk
  • 1 egg
  • 3 tbsp butter, melted
  • 1 tsp vanilla extract

Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, whisk together the flour, baking powder, salt, and sugar.
  2. Make a well in the center, then pour in the milk, egg, melted butter, and vanilla. Stir until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium-high heat.
  4. Pour 1/4 cup of batter onto the griddle for each pancake.
  5. Cook until bubbles form on the surface of the pancake, then flip and cook the other side until golden brown.
  6. Serve hot with syrup or toppings of choice.
