Spaghetti Carbonara

Servings: 4–6 | Prep Time: 10 min | Cook Time: 20 min

Pasta Italian Dinner

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp extra virgin olive oil or unsalted butter
  • 1/2 lb pancetta or thick cut bacon, diced
  • 1–2 garlic cloves, minced (optional)
  • 3–4 whole eggs
  • 1 cup grated Parmesan or Pecorino cheese
  • 1 lb spaghetti (or bucatini/fettuccine)
  • Kosher salt and black pepper to taste

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil.
  2. In a sauté pan over medium heat, cook pancetta or bacon until crispy. Add garlic (if using), sauté for 1 minute, then remove from heat.
  3. In a small bowl, beat eggs and mix in half of the cheese.
  4. Cook pasta until al dente. Reserve some pasta water, then drain.
  5. Transfer hot pasta to the pancetta bowl. Toss well to coat evenly.
  6. Quickly stir in egg mixture until creamy. Add pasta water if needed to loosen. Season with salt and pepper.
