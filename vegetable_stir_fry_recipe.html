Vegetable Stir Fry

Servings: 4 | Prep Time: 15 min | Cook Time: 10 min

Vegetarian Asian Quick & Easy

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 sliced red bell pepper
  • 1 sliced yellow bell pepper
  • 1 small sliced zucchini
  • 1 cup sugar snap peas
  • 1 cup sliced carrots
  • 1 cup sliced mushrooms
  • 2 cups broccoli
  • 1 (15-ounce) can baby corn, drained
  • 1 (8-ounce) can water chestnuts, drained
  • Green onions (for garnish)
  • Sesame seeds (for garnish)

Sauce

  • ¼ cup soy sauce
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 tbsp brown sugar
  • 1 tsp sesame oil
  • ½ cup chicken broth
  • 1 tbsp cornstarch

Instructions

  1. Heat olive oil in a wok or large skillet over medium-high heat.
  2. Add all vegetables. Cook for 2–3 minutes, stirring frequently.
  3. In a small bowl, whisk together soy sauce, garlic, brown sugar, sesame oil, broth, and cornstarch.
  4. Pour sauce over vegetables. Cook until sauce thickens and veggies are tender.
  5. Garnish with green onions and sesame seeds.
